About Us

We're dedicated to showcasing the incredible cultural, spiritual, and natural heritage of the Tricity region of Andhra Pradesh.

Celebrating the Heritage of Tricity

Tricity Tourism Hub was born from a passion to connect travelers with the extraordinary heritage of Guntur, Vijayawada, and Amaravathi — three cities that together form one of Andhra Pradesh's most historically rich regions.

From the divine hilltop Kanaka Durga Temple overlooking the Krishna River, to the 1,500-year-old rock-cut caves of Undavalli, and the pristine beaches of Suryalanka — our platform is your complete guide to experiencing the best of this magnificent region.

We offer destination guides, interactive maps, virtual tours, event listings, and seamless booking — all in one place, so you can plan your perfect Tricity journey.
