Vijayawada

Kanaka Durga Temple

Indrakeeladri Hill, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh

Timings

4:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Entry Fee

Free (Special Darshan ₹100)

Best Time

Oct - Mar

Distance

2 km from Vijayawada

About

The Kanaka Durga Temple is one of the most revered temples in South India, dedicated to Goddess Kanaka Durga. Located atop Indrakeeladri Hill, the temple offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Krishna River and Vijayawada city.

The temple has a rich history dating back centuries and is one of the Shakti Peethas. During Navaratri, the temple witnesses millions of devotees from across the country.

Highlights

  • Beautiful hilltop location with river views
  • Ancient Shakti Peetha temple
  • Special Navaratri celebrations
  • Rope-way facility available
  • Archaeological significance

How to Reach

Airport: Vijayawada Airport (20 km)
Railway: Vijayawada Junction (3 km)
Bus: Pandit Nehru Bus Station (2 km)
