About

Uppalapadu Bird Sanctuary is a haven for migratory birds and one of the most important bird-watching destinations in Andhra Pradesh.

The sanctuary is home to pelicans, storks, ibis, and several other species during the winter season.

Highlights

  • Migratory bird habitat
  • Pelicans and painted storks
  • Ideal for bird photography
  • Eco-tourism destination

How to Reach

Vijayawada Airport (55 km)
Guntur Railway Station (10 km)
APSRTC buses available
