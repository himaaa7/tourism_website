Kondapalli Fort

Kondapalli, Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh

Timings

Entry Fee

₹20

Best Time

Oct – Feb

Distance

16 km from Vijayawada

About

Kondapalli Fort is a historic fort built in the 14th century by the Musunuri Nayaks and later expanded by the Qutb Shahi rulers.

The fort is known for its massive gates, watchtowers, and its close association with the famous Kondapalli toy industry.

Highlights

  • Massive stone gateways
  • Hilltop fort with scenic views
  • Historic military architecture
  • Near Kondapalli toy village

How to Reach

Vijayawada Junction (16 km)
APSRTC buses from Vijayawada
