Undavalli Caves

Undavalli, Amaravathi, Andhra Pradesh

Timings

9:00 AM – 5:30 PM

Entry Fee

₹20

Best Time

Oct – Mar

Distance

10 km from Vijayawada

About

Undavalli Caves are ancient rock-cut caves dating back to the 4th–5th century, carved from solid sandstone.

The caves are renowned for the massive reclining statue of Lord Vishnu and their architectural brilliance.

Highlights

  • Rock-cut cave architecture
  • Reclining Vishnu sculpture
  • Archaeological significance
  • Scenic Krishna River views

How to Reach

Vijayawada Airport (20 km)
Vijayawada Junction (10 km)
APSRTC buses available
