Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our virtual tours

Are the virtual tours free?
Yes! All our video tours are free to watch. You can book a guided virtual tour session with a live expert guide for a more personalized experience.
What equipment do I need?
You only need a smartphone, tablet, or computer with a good internet connection. For 360° tours, a VR headset enhances the experience but is not required.
How does a guided virtual tour work?
After booking, our local expert guide conducts a live video call where they walk you through the destination using real-time video, sharing stories, history, and answering your questions.
Can I book a tour for a group?
Absolutely! We offer group guided tours for up to 20 participants on a shared video call. Larger groups can contact us for customized packages.
